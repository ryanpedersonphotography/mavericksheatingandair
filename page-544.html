Signs You Need an HVAC Repair in East Gull Lake

If your HVAC system is showing signs of trouble, it’s crucial to address these issues promptly. Early repairs can save you money and extend the lifespan of your unit. Here are some signs you may need to schedule an HVAC repair:

  • Frequent thermostat adjustments

  • Weak airflow

  • Uneven temperatures in your home

  • Unexpected spikes in energy bills

  • Strange sounds from your system

  • Excess dust in your home

  • Water leaks from units

  • Frozen evaporator coils

  • Inadequate cooling from air registers

No matter the problem, Mavericks Heating & Air has a solution. Our highly trained HVAC technicians are skilled in diagnosing and repairing all kinds of issues, from refrigerant leaks to malfunctioning thermostats.
