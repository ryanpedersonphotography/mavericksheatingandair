Key Features

Reliability and Durability
  • AirEase equipment is built to last. Our rigorous manufacturing processes ensure that each component withstands the test of time, providing homeowners with peace of mind.
  • From the robust cabinet construction to the high-quality materials used in coils and compressors, AirEase products are designed for longevity.
Energy Efficiency
  • AirEase systems are engineered to optimize energy usage. Our variable-speed technology adjusts output based on demand, reducing energy consumption and utility bills.
  • Look for the ENERGY STAR label on our products, indicating superior efficiency and environmental responsibility.
Quiet Operation
  • Nobody wants a noisy HVAC system disrupting their home. AirEase units feature advanced sound-dampening technology, ensuring whisper-quiet operation.
  • Whether its the outdoor condenser or the indoor air handler, youll hardly notice your AirEase system is running.
Advanced Comfort Control
  • AirEase thermostats and zoning systems allow precise temperature adjustments for different areas of your home.
  • Enjoy consistent comfort, whether youre relaxing in the living room or sleeping in the bedroom.
Warranty and Support
  • We stand behind our products. AirEase offers competitive warranties, providing coverage for parts and labor.
  • Our network of authorized dealers ensures prompt service and expert support whenever you need it.
Environmentally Friendly Practices
  • AirEase prioritizes sustainability. Our commitment to eco-friendly refrigerants and responsible manufacturing processes minimizes our environmental impact.
  • By choosing AirEase, youre contributing to a greener future.
Parts Warranty

AirEase offers a10-year limited warrantyon parts. This means that if any component of your AirEase system fails due to a manufacturing defect within the first 10 years, well cover the cost of replacement parts.

Compressor Warranty

Thecompressor, often referred to as the heart of your air conditioner or heat pump, is crucial for efficient operation. AirEase provides a10-year limited warrantyon compressors. If your compressor malfunctions during this period, well replace it at no additional cost.

Labor Allowance

In addition to parts and compressor coverage, AirEase offers a1-year labor allowance. This means that during the first year after installation, if you need a professional technician to service your AirEase equipment, well contribute towards the labor costs.

Extended Warranty Options

For added peace of mind, consider ourextended warranty options. These can extend coverage beyond the initial warranty period, ensuring continued protection for your investment.

Remember that proper installation and regular maintenance are essential for maintaining warranty coverage. Be sure to register your AirEase product promptly after installation to activate the warranty. For specific details and terms, consult your authorized AirEase dealer or visit our website.

Choose AirEase with confidence, knowing that we stand behind our products and prioritize your comfort and satisfaction!
